Music

The Music package defines structures for describing music agnostic to methods of graphical or sonic representation.

The Music package contains several modules:

  • Pitch
  • MetricalDuration
  • Rhythm
  • Meter
  • Tempo
  • Dynamics
  • Articulations
  • MusicModel
