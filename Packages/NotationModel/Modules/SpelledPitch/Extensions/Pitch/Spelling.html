Fine Adjustment
  • EighthStepModifier

    Fine resolution component of a Pitch.Spelling. Analogous to an up or down (or lack of) arrow of an accidental.

    Note

    In 48-EDO, represents 1/8th-tone adjustment. May be useful for other cases (e.g., -14c adjustment for 5th partial, etc.).
