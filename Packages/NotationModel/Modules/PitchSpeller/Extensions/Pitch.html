  • resolution

    Fineness of Pitch.

  • 1.00: half-tone (e.g., c natural, g sharp, etc.)
  • 0.50: quarter-tone (e.g., c quarterShap, g quarterFlat, etc.)

  • 0.25: eighth-tone (e.g., c natural up, q quarterflat down, etc.)
